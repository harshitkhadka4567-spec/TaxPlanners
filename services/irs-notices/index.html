IRS and state notices

Notice review and response support

Support for reviewing IRS and state notices, organizing response information, and handling tax correspondence in a structured way.

Back to Services

How we help

We review notices, identify requested information, support response preparation, and assist with Power of Attorney or IRS Form 2848 when applicable.

Common situations

Balance due notices, missing information requests, state correspondence, filing questions, payment issues, and tax account follow-up.

What to prepare

The complete notice, related tax year records, prior correspondence, proof of payment if relevant, and a copy of the return involved.
