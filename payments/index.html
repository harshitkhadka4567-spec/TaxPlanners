Important security notice

This website does not collect or store credit card numbers, bank information, Social Security numbers, ITINs, tax documents, IDs, or sensitive financial documents. Online payments should be completed only through the secure payment link provided by the office.

Pay Through QuickBooks Payments

Clients may pay securely through QuickBooks Payments when a payment link or invoice payment page is provided by the office. Please include your invoice number, name, or account reference when submitting payment.

Where to Find Your Fee

Your preparation fee is usually listed on the first page of your tax return package or invoice. Please review your package before contacting the office. If you are unsure, contact the office with your name and invoice number.

Credit Card Processing Notice

Credit card payments may include a 4% processing surcharge where permitted. Any surcharge will be clearly disclosed before payment and will not exceed the processing cost charged to the firm. Clients who prefer to avoid a credit card processing surcharge may pay by check where available.
